# Legion Buildings

## Command Center
- main Legion structure
- produces structures and defences
- uses off-field plus trickle structure production
- uses the shared global ProductionQueue HUD feature (available to all factions)

## Power Plant
- cost: 4 credits
- build_time: 5 s
- energy_provided: 5

## Barracks
- cost: 4 credits
- build_time: 6 s
- energy_required: 5
- produces infantry

## Refinery
- cost: 2000 credits
- build_time: 20 s
- energy_required: 50
- deploys a Harvester for the Legion economy loop
- Harvester cargo is delivered to the nearest valid drop-off structure

## Assembly
- cost: 2000 credits
- build_time: 20 s
- energy_required: 50
- produces vehicles

## Airfield
- cost: 2000 credits
- build_time: 20 s
- energy_required: 50
- produces aircraft
