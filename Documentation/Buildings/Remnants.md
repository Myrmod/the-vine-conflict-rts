# Buildings:
## HQ:
- Main Remnants structure
- Produces structures and defences
- Uses off-field plus trickle structure production
- Uses the shared global ProductionQueue HUD feature (available to all factions)

## Power plant: 
- Costs 600 , 6 secs build time 
- Provides 150 power

## Burn Harvest System:
- Remnants do not use a refinery return loop.
- They gain resources by burning and destroying ResourceVines.
- If a tile is being burned, it is considered actively harvested.
- Gather rate is measured in destroyed resources per tick.
- When a burned tile is depleted, Remnants gatherers automatically retarget the next available resource tile.

## Gather Units:
- Incinerator (infantry)
	- Produced in Barracks
- Flame Tank (vehicle)
	- Produced in Vehicle Bay

## Casern:
- costs 600, 6 sec build time, costs 25 power
- Produces infantry

## Factory:
- costs 2000, 20 sec build time, costs 50 power
- Produces vehicles

## Drone Tower:
- costs 2000, 20 sec build time, costs 50 power
- Produces air units

## Naval yard:
- costs 1500, 15 sec build time, costs 50 power
- Produces ships
