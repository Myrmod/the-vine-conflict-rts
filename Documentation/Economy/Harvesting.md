# Harvesting mechanics:
## corta vine:
- they use refineries are normal harveters
- harvestering stats:
  - capacity:250
  - speed:2
  - harvest rate:100 res per second
  - delivery rate: 100 res per second

## pro vines:
- they use refineries are normal harveters
- harvestering stats:
  - capacity:500
  - speed:1
  - harvest rate:100 res per second
  - delivery rate: 100 res per second

## plant faction:
- they use building to harvest energy from vines,harvests in a radius around it 3 tiles
- harvesting stats:
 - capacity: infinite
 - speed:0
 - harvester rate:75 res per second
 - deelivery rate: 75 per second
 - boost feild growth per 5 % per building

## The Amuns:
- they terraform vines into amuns matter by building refinery nodes that can build obelisks.
- refinery nodes buffs units around it
- nodes harvest in a radius of 5 tiles
- harvesting stats:
- capacity: infinite
- speed:0
- harvest rate:100 per second( gets bonus 50 res) 
- delivery rate: same
- has a turret for self defence
